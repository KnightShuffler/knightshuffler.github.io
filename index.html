About Me

I am a computer science graduate with an interest in quantum computing, theoretical computer science and computer graphics.

Being able to make my own video games was the only motivation I needed to learn how to program. From tinkering with Game Maker to my computer science classes in high school where I learned C++, I had learned enough to make my first game entirely from scratch. Swine Flew was a simple horizontal side-scroller where the player controls a flying pig.

Since then, I have dabbled in graphics programming in OpenGL and GPGPU programming in CUDA.
